01 · About

A little about me.

I am a PhD student at XYZ University, advised by Prof. A and Prof. B. Before this, I received my Bachelor's degree from ABC University, where I worked with Prof. C on probabilistic inference.

My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, natural language processing, and human–computer interaction. I am especially interested in models that can learn from feedback, reason step-by-step, and collaborate with people on complex tasks.

Outside of research, I enjoy long-distance running, fountain pens, and reading non-fiction.
